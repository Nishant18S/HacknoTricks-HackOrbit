Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a government scheme?

You can apply online through this portal by selecting your desired scheme, filling out the application form, and uploading the required documents.

What documents are required?

Required documents typically include proof of identity, proof of address, income proof, and scheme-specific documents. The exact list will be shown during the application process.

How long does approval take?

Approval time varies by scheme and completeness of documentation, but most applications are processed within 7-14 working days.
