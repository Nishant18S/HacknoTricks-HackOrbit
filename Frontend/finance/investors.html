India's GDP Growth Story

Consistent Growth

India's GDP has grown from $1.9 trillion in 2015 to $3.7 trillion in 2025, averaging 7% annual growth.

Per Capita Income

Per capita income has doubled from $1,500 in 2015 to $3,000 in 2025, reflecting broad-based growth.

Sector Contribution

Services (55%), Industry (27%), and Agriculture (18%) form the three pillars of India's GDP.

Global Standing

India is now the 5th largest economy globally and projected to be 3rd largest by 2030.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can invest in government projects?

Both domestic and foreign investors can participate in government-backed investment opportunities, subject to eligibility and regulatory requirements.

What are the minimum investment amounts?

Minimum investment amounts vary by project. Please check the details for each opportunity.

How are returns on government investments calculated?

Returns are project-specific and may include fixed interest, profit-sharing, or capital gains, as outlined in each investment prospectus.

Can I withdraw my investment early?

Early withdrawal options and penalties, if any, are specified in the terms of each investment.
