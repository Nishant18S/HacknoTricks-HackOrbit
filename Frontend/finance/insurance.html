Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for government insurance programs?

All Indian citizens are eligible for government insurance programs, with some schemes targeting specific groups such as farmers, senior citizens, or low-income families.

What documents are required?

Commonly required documents include proof of identity, proof of address, age proof, and income proof. Specific programs may require additional documents.

How long does it take to get coverage?

Coverage begins after your application is approved and the first premium is paid. Most applications are processed within 7-14 working days.

Can I claim for pre-existing conditions?

Some health insurance programs cover pre-existing conditions after a waiting period. Please check the specific policy details for more information.
