üìö Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my application status?

You can track your application status by going to the Applications section in your dashboard. Each application shows its current status and any required actions.

What documents are required for loan applications?

Required documents include Aadhaar card, PAN card, income certificate, bank statements, and collateral documents (if applicable). Check the specific loan page for detailed requirements.

How long does application processing take?

Processing times vary by service: Loans (15-30 days), Insurance (7-14 days), Schemes (10-21 days). You'll receive updates via email and SMS.

Can I edit my application after submission?

Applications can be edited within 24 hours of submission if they're still in "Pending" status. After that, you'll need to contact support for changes.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password from the login page using the "Forgot Password" link, or change it from the Settings section in your dashboard.
