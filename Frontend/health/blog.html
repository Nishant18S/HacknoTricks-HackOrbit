Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common health questions and concerns. If you don't find what you're looking for, contact our medical team.

What are the visiting hours at your hospital? +
Our hospital visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. However, ICU visiting hours are restricted to 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24/7. We recommend calling ahead to confirm specific department timings.
How do I book an appointment online? +
You can book appointments through our website by clicking the "Book Appointment" button, calling our helpline at +91-102, or using our mobile app. Online booking is available 24/7, and you'll receive confirmation via SMS and email.
What insurance plans do you accept? +
We accept all major insurance plans including Ayushman Bharat, CGHS, ESIC, and private insurance providers. We also offer cashless treatment for eligible policyholders. Please bring your insurance card and valid ID for verification.
Do you provide emergency services? +
Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services with a dedicated emergency department, trauma center, and ambulance services. Our emergency team includes specialists in critical care, surgery, and emergency medicine.
What COVID-19 safety measures are in place? +
We follow strict COVID-19 protocols including mandatory mask wearing, temperature screening, sanitization, social distancing, and separate OPD timings for COVID and non-COVID patients. All staff are fully vaccinated and regularly tested.
How can I get my medical records? +
Medical records can be requested at the Medical Records Department with valid ID proof. Digital copies are available through our patient portal. Processing time is typically 2-3 working days for physical records and immediate for digital access.
Do you offer telemedicine consultations? +
Yes, we offer telemedicine consultations for routine follow-ups, prescription renewals, and non-emergency medical advice. Book online or call our telemedicine helpline. Video consultations are available with most of our specialists.
What are your laboratory testing hours? +
Our laboratory is open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM on weekdays and 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekends. Fasting blood tests are preferred before 10:00 AM. Emergency lab services are available 24/7 for inpatients and critical cases.
