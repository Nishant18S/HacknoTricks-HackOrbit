Doctor Services Support

How do I find doctors by specialty?
Use our advanced filter system to search by specialty, location, experience, and patient ratings. Click on "Find Doctors" and select your preferred filters.
Can I view doctor profiles before booking?
Yes! Each doctor has a detailed profile showing qualifications, experience, patient reviews, consultation fees, and available time slots.
How do I check doctor availability?
Real-time availability is shown on each doctor's profile. Green slots indicate available times, and you can book instantly.
What if I need to change my preferred doctor?
You can easily switch doctors before confirming your appointment. After booking, you can reschedule with a different doctor if needed.

Appointment Management

How do I book an appointment?
Select your doctor, choose an available time slot, fill in your details, and confirm. You'll receive instant confirmation via SMS and email.
Can I reschedule or cancel appointments?
Yes, you can reschedule or cancel up to 2 hours before your appointment time through your account dashboard or by calling our helpline.
Do I get appointment reminders?
Yes! We send SMS and email reminders 24 hours and 2 hours before your appointment, plus push notifications if you have our app.
What about emergency appointments?
For urgent cases, call our emergency line. We'll help you find the nearest available doctor or direct you to emergency services.

Medical Records & Privacy

How do I access my medical records?
Log into your secure account using your credentials. All your medical history, test results, and prescriptions are stored safely and accessible 24/7.
Is my medical data secure?
Absolutely! We use bank-level encryption and comply with all healthcare data protection regulations. Your data is never shared without your explicit consent.
Can I upload my own medical documents?
Yes, you can securely upload lab reports, prescriptions, X-rays, and other medical documents. Supported formats include PDF, JPG, and PNG.
How do I share records with my doctor?
You can easily share specific records with your healthcare providers through our secure sharing system. You control what gets shared and with whom.

Pharmacy & Medicine Orders

How do I order medicines online?
Upload your prescription, search for medicines, add to cart, and checkout. We verify prescriptions with licensed pharmacists before dispatch.
Do you deliver to my location?
We deliver to most cities and towns. Enter your PIN code to check delivery availability and estimated delivery time in your area.
What if a medicine is out of stock?
We'll immediately notify you and suggest alternatives. Our pharmacists can recommend generic equivalents or help you find the medicine at nearby pharmacies.
Can I find nearby physical pharmacies?
Yes! Use our pharmacy locator to find nearby stores, check their stock availability, and get directions. Many partner pharmacies offer discounts too.
